How It Works

Three simple steps to save lives

1

Hospital Sends SOS

Hospital sends an emergency request through our system when blood is urgently needed.

2

Instant Notifications

LifeLink notifies nearby compatible donors and blood banks in real-time.

3

Donor Responds
